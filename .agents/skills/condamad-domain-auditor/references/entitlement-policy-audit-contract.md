# Entitlement Policy Audit Contract

Audit must verify:

- single canonical policy owner;
- no direct entitlement bypass;
- mutation lifecycle;
- audit trail;
- review/approval state transitions;
- temporal validity;
- negative tests for unauthorized mutation;
- consistency between current state, history, and rules.

Required checks:

- policy owner inventory;
- bypass scan for direct mutations;
- mutation lifecycle trace;
- audit trail evidence;
- negative authorization test inventory.
