# Executive Summary

The CS-346 audit is closed for the natal astrology input source domain. `llm_astrology_input_v1` is assembled by one canonical builder from `structured_facts_v1`, `AINarrativeInputContract`, and `client_interpretation_projection_v1`. The modern prompt-visible surface is limited to `facts`, `signals`, `limits`, and `shaping`; evidence, provenance, hashes, validation owner, provider response, and persisted answer remain backend-only. `chart_json` and `natal_data` are classified as forbidden legacy carriers for the modern natal prompt and are covered by passing guards.
